What is DSC?

DSC is to help solve the challenges university graduates were facing securing employment in the tech industry. We aimed to provide students with the resources, opportunities and, experience necessary to be industry ready, all while still pursuing their degrees.

Why DSC?

Developer Students Club-NIT Surat is a Google recognized student body at NIT-Surat which directly reports to Google Developers for their activities and is supported by the tech giant. DSC-SVNIT conducts workshops, seminars and other fun activities which help the students connect with Google.
